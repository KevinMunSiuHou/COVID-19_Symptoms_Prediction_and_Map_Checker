Background

The spread of COVID-19 has severely taken affair in over 200 countries worldwide. The virus could lead to an effect from mild to moderate upper-respiratory tract illnesses for instance Severe Acute Respiratory Syndrome (SARS), Middle East respiratory syndrome (MERS) and Common Cold
